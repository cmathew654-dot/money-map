# Demo provenance

## Synthetic identity

Cairn is a fictional product identity. The Hartwell family and every household-specific value, date, relationship, note, instruction, and scenario in the four starters are synthetic and illustrative. They do not describe a real household, advisor, firm, custodian relationship, recommendation, or transaction.

The application is a communication and authoring demonstration. Financial-looking content is literal display text, not calculated output or advice.

## Reference-material handling

Reference material informed the broad problem framing and the choice to replace slide-based financial diagrams with a purpose-built canvas. Public implementation assets and compositions were created for this repository. Raw reference captures are not committed, redistributed, embedded, or served by the application.

Design details that could identify a client, advisor, firm, or source document were excluded rather than anonymized into the demo. The four public starters use a fictional household, original copy, and repository-owned styling.

## Excluded artifacts

The tracked public tree excludes:

- real client or household information;
- real firm logos, brand assets, or proprietary templates;
- raw reference screenshots, slide decks, recordings, and exports;
- local environment files, credentials, and editor state;
- generated `dist`, coverage, Playwright report, screenshot, and test-result directories;
- archives and internal agent/workflow reports.

These exclusions are reinforced by repository hygiene rules and a tracked-file audit. If reference material is needed for future design work, keep it outside the repository and translate only non-identifying product requirements into original fixtures, tests, and implementation.
